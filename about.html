About DiscoverVN

🧭

Our Mission

We believe travel should enrich both the visitor and the destination. DiscoverVN was founded to create honest, in-depth guides that help travellers connect with Vietnam's real culture — its food, its people, and its landscapes — without contributing to over-tourism.

🌿

Responsible Travel

Every destination we recommend is assessed for its environmental impact and community benefit. We partner exclusively with locally owned guesthouses, guides, and restaurants, ensuring your travel spending stays in the communities you visit.

📖

Authentic Expertise

Our team of writers lives in and across Vietnam. We don't write about places we haven't visited ourselves — each recommendation is backed by on-the-ground research, local knowledge, and honest experience.

How It Started

Born From a Broken Guidebook and a Motorcycle

In 2018, our founder Minh rode a 125cc motorbike from Hanoi to Hồ Chí Minh City along Highway 1. With a guidebook three years out of date and a phone with a broken GPS, he discovered something invaluable: the best experiences in Vietnam are never in any guidebook.

A noodle stall in a Hue side-street. A family-run homestay above the clouds in Mù Cang Chải. A fishing village on Phú Quốc that hadn't yet been discovered by package tours. These were the real Vietnam.

DiscoverVN was built to share exactly those kinds of places — the ones locals love, the ones that haven't been filtered through the lens of mass tourism. We are a small team with a big passion, and this website is our ongoing love letter to one of the most extraordinary countries on Earth.
